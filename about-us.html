OUR ORGANIZATION

Trichy Wood Plast was established with a vision to provide high-quality, durable interior solutions. Currently, we are a leading provider of premium doors and furniture sections, backed by decades of expertise and dedication.

Our area of competence is producing a broad range of profiles, including uPVC Doors, Sections, Frames, Kitchen Furniture, Wardrobes, and many more. We put a lot of effort into maintaining the value of materials by offering our customers the most suitable surface solutions.

Using cutting-edge manufacturing technologies and quality assurance inspections will ensure that all our products are delivered on schedule and under budget. We aim to respect your materials and serve as your go-to source for ethical surface solutions.

To do this, our team constantly innovates and collaborates with partners who share our perspective on the market. We concentrate our efforts on people, aiming to achieve the following objectives:

01.
To collaborate to lower our energy usage as well as our carbon emissions.
02.
We respect those who work with us, both men and women. We pay close attention to the working conditions of our staff.
03.
To ensure that they satisfy the necessary safety and performance standards, all of our products go through a thorough quality testing process.
