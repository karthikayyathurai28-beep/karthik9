FAQs About Home Interior Design

1. Why do I need an interior designer? +

You can think of an interior designer as a film director. Their job is to monitor every minute detail from start to the final cut. Similarly, interior designers are professionals who are able to gauge your needs and tastes to deliver your dream home.

2. Why is Trichy Wood Plast perfect for your home interior design? +

We combine 35+ years of experience with modern WPC technology. Our doors and interior solutions are durable, termite-proof, and waterproof, making them ideal for long-lasting home interiors in any environment.

3. What services are included under home interior design? +

Our services include modular kitchens, wardrobe designs, TV units, wall paneling, and custom WPC door installations. We provide end-to-end solutions from design consultation to post-installation service.

4. How much do home interiors cost? +

The cost varies based on the scope of work and materials chosen. However, we offer transparent pricing and flexible packages to suit different budget ranges without compromising on quality.

5. What will be the timelines for my project completion? +

We pride ourselves on our 45-day delivery guarantee. Most standard interior projects are completed within this timeframe once the designs are finalized.

6. What are the trending interior design styles? +

Currently, minimalist designs, sustainable materials (like WPC), and smart-space utilization are very popular. We offer over 600+ design expert consultations to help you choose the best trend for your home.
