FAQs About Our Doors

1. What is a Hybrid Material Door? +

Hybrid Material Doors (WPC) combine the strength of wood with the durability of synthetic polymers. This results in a door that looks like wood but is completely waterproof and termite proof.

2. Are Trichy Wood Plast doors truly termite proof? +

Yes! Our doors are 100% termite and borer proof. Unlike traditional wood, the hybrid material does not attract insects, ensuring your doors last for decades without any pest damage.

3. Do these doors require painting or polishing? +

Our doors come with a factory-finished, high-quality surface that does not require any additional painting or polishing on-site. They are ready to install and easy to maintain.

4. Can I use these doors for bathrooms and kitchens? +

Absolutely. Since our doors are 100% waterproof and moisture-resistant, they are the perfect choice for high-humidity areas like bathrooms and kitchens where traditional wood would rot.

5. What is the warranty on Trichy Wood Plast doors? +

We offer a flat 10-year warranty on our door products, covering quality defects and material integrity. We stand by the durability of our modern craftsmanship.

6. Are custom sizes and designs available? +

Yes, we offer a wide range of standard sizes, and we can also accommodate custom size requests (within min/max limits) and specific designer finishes to match your interior theme.
